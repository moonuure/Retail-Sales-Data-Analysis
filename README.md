# Retail Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail sales dataset to understand patterns in **sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and sales channels**.

The objective of the analysis is to extract meaningful insights that can support **data-driven business decision-making**.

Through **data cleaning, feature engineering, and exploratory data analysis (EDA)**, the project identifies key factors that influence revenue generation and profitability.

---

# Dataset Description

## Dataset Source

The dataset used in this project was obtained from **Kaggle**, a popular platform for data science and machine learning datasets.

Kaggle provides publicly available datasets that are commonly used for **data analysis practice, machine learning experiments, and research projects**.  

The retail sales dataset used in this analysis contains **1000 sales transaction records** and includes information about product categories, sales representatives, regions, customer types, payment methods, discounts, and sales channels.

Using this dataset allowed the project to simulate a **real-world retail business environment** and perform meaningful sales performance analysis.


### Key Variables

* **Product_ID** – Unique identifier for each product
* **Sale_Date** – Date of the transaction
* **Sales_Rep** – Sales representative responsible for the sale
* **Region** – Sales region
* **Sales_Amount** – Total value of the sale
* **Quantity_Sold** – Number of units sold
* **Product_Category** – Product classification
* **Unit_Cost** – Cost per unit
* **Unit_Price** – Selling price per unit
* **Customer_Type** – New or returning customer
* **Discount** – Discount applied to the transaction
* **Payment_Method** – Method used for payment
* **Sales_Channel** – Online or retail channel

# Key Insights

### Regional Sales Performance

The **North region generated the highest sales**, indicating stronger market demand.

### Product Category Performance

The **Furniture category produced the highest profit**, making it the most profitable category.

### Sales Representative Performance

**David achieved the highest total sales**, demonstrating the strongest performance among sales representatives.

### Customer Behavior

Returning customers generated slightly more revenue than new customers, highlighting the importance of **customer retention strategies**.

### Payment Method Trends

**Credit card payments were the most commonly used payment method**, indicating a strong preference for digital transactions.

### Discount Impact

The correlation between discount and sales amount was **extremely weak (0.02)**, suggesting discounts have minimal influence on sales performance.

### Sales Channel Performance

**Retail sales slightly outperformed online sales**, although both channels contribute significantly to overall revenue.

# Business Recommendations

Based on the findings, the following strategic recommendations are proposed:

### Focus on High-Profit Categories

Businesses should prioritize products in the **Furniture category** due to their high profitability.

### Strengthen Customer Retention

Implement loyalty programs and personalized marketing to increase repeat purchases from returning customers.

### Optimize Discount Strategies

Since discounts have minimal impact on sales, businesses should review discount policies to avoid unnecessary revenue loss.

### Improve Sales Representative Performance

Provide training and performance incentives for lower-performing sales representatives.

### Expand High-Performing Regions

Increase marketing investment and inventory allocation in regions with strong sales performance.

### Maintain Multi-Channel Sales

Continue investing in both **online and retail sales channels** to maximize customer reach.

# Project Conclusion

This project analyzed retail sales data to identify patterns in **sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability**.

Through exploratory data analysis, several key insights were identified:

* Product category and region have stronger influence on revenue than discount levels.
* The **Furniture category generated the highest profit**.
* **Customer retention plays an important role in revenue generation**.

These findings can help businesses improve **strategic decision-making, sales performance, and product management**.
